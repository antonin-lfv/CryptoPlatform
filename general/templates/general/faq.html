What is Crypto Platforme?
Crypto Platforme is a comprehensive platform that simulates a complete cryptocurrency and NFT ecosystem, including trading, mining, and an NFT marketplace.
How do I get started on Crypto Platforme?
Start by accessing your virtual USD crypto wallet, explore the NFT market, and the mining section. Trading will soon be available to enhance your interaction with the ecosystem.
How does the virtual wallet work?
You are provided with an initial balance in virtual USD. You can buy Bitcoin, then convert it into 9 other cryptocurrencies, including a stablecoin. The balance is updated in real-time with widgets.
When will trading be available?
The trading functionality is currently under development. It will allow you to 'bet' your tokens on the fluctuation of cryptocurrency values using a leverage system, with advanced features like stop loss and stop win.
How can I acquire NFTs on the platform?
NFTs can be purchased with ETH. Each NFT is unique and belongs to a specific collection. The price varies with ETH fluctuations. A dedicated marketplace facilitates buying, selling, and bidding.
Can I use an NFT as my avatar?
Yes, users can use the NFTs they own as avatars on their profile on the platform. This feature is available in the NFT details page.
How does mining work on Crypto Platforme?
You can buy or rent servers to mine different cryptocurrencies. Each server has specific costs and yields. Mining revenues are automatically added to your wallet.
Can I sell my mining servers?
Yes, servers can be sold on the platform's marketplace, allowing you to recover part of your initial investment or make profits based on market demand and prices.
